
BUSINESS INSIGHTS - OLIST CHURN RISK MODEL
===========================================

1. VENDOR DISPATCH TIME (days_to_carrier) - MOST CRITICAL
   Vendors that take longer to ship products are the #1 driver of churn risk.
   Recommendation: Implement SLA alerts for vendors exceeding 2 days to dispatch.

2. SEASONALITY (purchase_month)
   Orders placed in Q4 (Oct-Dec) have significantly higher churn risk due to
   logistics overload during Black Friday and Christmas periods.
   Recommendation: Increase logistics capacity and set conservative delivery
   estimates during peak months.

3. ORDER SIZE (n_items)
   Multi-item orders carry higher churn risk, likely due to higher chance of
   partial delivery issues.
   Recommendation: Prioritize quality checks for orders with 3+ items.

4. FREIGHT COST (total_freight + freight_ratio)
   High freight costs increase churn risk by raising customer expectations.
   Recommendation: Review freight pricing strategy for long-distance deliveries.

5. INTERSTATE DELIVERIES (interstate)
   Cross-state deliveries show higher churn risk than local ones.
   Recommendation: Partner with regional carriers to improve last-mile delivery.
